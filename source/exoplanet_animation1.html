The animation representation of other planets

Gas Giants: Massive planets primarily composed of hydrogen and helium. Ice Giants: Planets with icy compositions, similar to Uranus and Neptune. Super-Earths: Rocky planets larger than Earth but smaller than Neptune. Mini-Neptunes: Planets with sizes between Earth and Neptune, often with thick atmospheres. Hot Jupiters: Gas giants orbiting extremely close to their stars. Rocky Planets: Earth-like worlds with solid surfaces. Ocean Worlds: Planets potentially covered in global oceans.
