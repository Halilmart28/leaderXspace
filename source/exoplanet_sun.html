The representation of the sizes of the outer planets as an animation

The representation of the sizes of the outer planets as an animation is a dynamic visual tool used to illustrate the scale and proportions of the gas and ice giants in our solar system. This type of animation typically focuses on Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune, showcasing their immense size compared to Earth and each other.
