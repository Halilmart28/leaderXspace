Compare the sizes of various exoplanets in an animated format.

Comparing the sizes of various exoplanets in an animated format is a powerful educational tool that helps visualize the incredible diversity of planets discovered beyond our solar system. This type of animation serves multiple purposes: Scale Representation: It provides a clear visual comparison of exoplanet sizes, often using familiar objects like Earth or Jupiter as reference points. Diversity Showcase: The animation can highlight a wide range of exoplanet types, from small rocky worlds to massive gas giants and everything in between.
